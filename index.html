Our Story

Where Cocktails Take Flight

Perched on the 3rd floor of SLN Terminus, Chirp is Hyderabad's most immersive avian-inspired craft cocktail experience.

Founder Nimit Gupta envisioned a space unlike anything Hyderabad had seen — a celebration of nature, innovation, and community that goes far beyond a traditional bar. Every detail at Chirp channels the beauty of the avian world: bird-cage seating draped in lush greenery, bespoke chandeliers, feathered wall art, and live canaries that add a touch of magic to your evening.

Gazing out over the lush stretch of Hyderabad's Botanical Garden, our skilled mixologists craft each drink using molecular techniques — smoke infusion, liquid nitrogen, seasonal local ingredients sourced directly from community farmers. At Chirp, every sip is a revelation and every visit an adventure.

"At Chirp, we're thrilled to introduce a one-of-a-kind experience that goes beyond traditional mixology. Chirp is not just a bar; it's a celebration of nature, innovation, and community."

— Nimit Gupta, Founder
